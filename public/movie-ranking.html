The Best Movies According to Brandon

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Toy Story

This movie is very nostalgic for me and Pixar just does really well with all the characters and storylines.

Dune 2

Although it is 3 hours long, this movie was set up perfectly and is filled with great storywriting, action, and drama. One of the better recent sci-fi films to date.

Everything Everywhere All At Once

Another 2+ hour movie but this movie packs an amazing story filled with laughter, action, and tears as it tells a heart warming story through multiversal adeventures.
